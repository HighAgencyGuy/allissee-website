Our Story

A legacy of excellence in palm oil production

Dr. Frederick Aya's Vision

In 2003, Dr. Frederick Aya embarked on a mission to revolutionize palm oil production in Africa. With a Ph.D. in Agricultural Sciences and years of experience in sustainable farming, he founded Allissee & Company with a clear vision: to combine traditional farming wisdom with modern agricultural technology.

What started as a modest 50-hectare farm has grown into one of Africa's leading palm oil producers, with over 1,000 hectares of sustainably managed plantations and a state-of-the-art seedling nursery.

Our Mission

To lead Africa's agricultural transformation through sustainable palm oil production and innovative seedling development, while empowering local communities and preserving our environment for future generations.
